Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2023

World Time: Tell Time ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to respecting and protecting the privacy of users ("you", "your") of our World Time: Tell Time mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our App.

By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) or non-personally identifiable information (non-PII) from users of the App. However, the App stores data about your selected timezones locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to us or any third parties.

MapLibre SDK

The App uses the MapLibre SDK to display map data about timezones. MapLibre is an open-source library that provides mapping services. While we do not collect or store any data through the use of MapLibre SDK, you are encouraged to review MapLibre's Privacy Policy to understand their data collection and usage practices.

Log Data

The App does not generate, collect, or store any log data.

Cookies

The App does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.

Service Providers

We may employ third-party libraries, such as MapLibre SDK, to facilitate the functionality of our App. MapLibre SDK is an open-source library that provides mapping services, and we use it to display map data about timezones. While we do not collect or store any data through the use of MapLibre SDK, you are encouraged to review MapLibre's Privacy Policy to understand their data collection and usage practices.

Security

We take the privacy and security of your information seriously. Since the App does not transmit or store any user data externally, there is no risk of unauthorized access, use, disclosure, or modification of your personal information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
